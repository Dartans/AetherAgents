Simple Steps to Intelligent Results with Aether Agents.

Unlock the power of LLMs for your business in just three easy steps. From purchasing hourly blocks to receiving intelligent outputs, we've made AI intermediation seamless.

Step 1: Choose Your Hourly Blocks

Browse our flexible hourly packages on the Pricing page and select the one that best suits your needs. We offer a range of packages to accommodate projects of all sizes. Our online purchase process is secure and straightforward, accepting major credit cards and payment methods.

Once you've selected your package, you'll receive immediate access to our platform to begin defining your tasks.

Step 2: Define Your Task Clearly

Access our user-friendly platform after purchase. Here, you'll provide detailed information about your business task. The clearer and more specific your instructions, the better the results our Aether Agents can deliver.

Our task submission form guides you through the process, prompting you for essential details such as:

  • Task Type: (e.g., Content Creation, Customer Service, Data Analysis)
  • Specific Instructions: Detailed description of what you need the Aether Agent to do.
  • Input Data (if applicable): Upload datasets, documents, or provide relevant links.
  • Desired Output Format: Specify how you want the results delivered (e.g., text document, spreadsheet, report).

Step 3: Receive Your Intelligent Outputs

Now, our Aether Agents get to work! Leveraging the power of large language models, they intelligently process your task instructions and input data. You'll receive your intelligent results directly within the platform, ready to use in your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of tasks can Aether Agents handle?

Aether Agents are versatile and can handle a wide range of business tasks, particularly those involving language understanding and generation. Our initial focus is on Customer Service Intermediation, Content Creation, and Data Analysis. However, the capabilities are constantly expanding. Contact us to discuss your specific task needs.

How are hourly blocks calculated?

Hourly blocks represent the estimated processing time for Aether Agents to complete tasks. The actual time may vary depending on task complexity. We strive to provide accurate estimates based on typical task parameters. We'll provide guidance on package selection based on your project needs.

What if I need more hours than initially purchased?

You can easily purchase additional hourly blocks as needed. If you anticipate ongoing or high-volume usage, our Enterprise Packages or custom solutions may offer better value.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide email support for all packages. Professional and Enterprise packages include priority support with faster response times. For Enterprise clients, we offer dedicated account management and more personalized support.
