Become an Aether Agent - Showcase Your LLM Expertise.

Join the future of intelligent intermediation and demonstrate your LLM skills to businesses seeking AI-powered solutions.

Create Your Aether Agent Profile Page

As part of the application process, you are required to create your own Aether Agent Profile Page hosted on GitHub Pages. This page serves as your professional showcase and demonstrates your LLM capabilities to our team.

**Your Profile Page Must Include:**

  • Professional Design: Ensure your profile page has a clean, professional, and visually appealing design. This is your first impression!
  • Profile Picture: A professional headshot or avatar representing you.
  • Name: Your full name or professional alias.
  • Country: Your country of residence.
  • Knowledge Domains: Clearly list your areas of expertise and knowledge relevant to LLM applications (e.g., Content Creation, Customer Service, Data Analysis, specific industries, etc.).
  • Credentials: Highlight relevant certifications, educational background, or professional experience that demonstrates your skills and credibility.
  • Links to Past Projects: Showcase examples of your past work, especially projects demonstrating your LLM skills. This could include links to GitHub repositories, demos, or case studies.
  • LLM Chat Link: **Crucially, embed a link to a live LLM-powered chat application that you have used to help create your profile.** This chat should effectively demonstrate your LLM skills in a practical and interactive way. Use Google Gemini or a similar LLM.

Your profile page hosted on GitHub Pages link and the LLM chat link must be submitted as part of your application below.

What is an Aether Agent?

Aether Agents are skilled professionals who act as intermediaries between businesses and the power of Large Language Models (LLMs). You will be instrumental in translating business tasks into effective LLM prompts and workflows, ensuring our clients get the best possible results from AI-powered solutions. This role combines understanding business needs with the ability to effectively leverage advanced AI technologies.

What We Look For in Aether Agents

  • LLM Skill Demonstration: Your profile page and LLM Chat Link are key to showcasing your practical LLM skills.
  • Strong Communication Skills: Ability to clearly understand business requirements and translate them into actionable tasks for LLMs.
  • Problem-Solving Abilities: Creativity and resourcefulness in finding effective solutions using LLMs for diverse business challenges.
  • Adaptability and Learning Agility: The AI landscape is rapidly evolving. A willingness to learn new tools and techniques is essential.
  • Experience in Relevant Fields: Experience in areas like customer service, content writing, data analysis, or related business domains is beneficial, and strictly required.
  • Genuine Interest in AI and its Business Applications: A passion for AI and a desire to help businesses succeed with intelligent automation.

Application Process

  1. Create Your Agent Profile Page: Build your profile page on GitHub Pages, ensuring it meets all the requirements outlined above, including the LLM Chat Link.
  2. Submit Your Application: Complete the form below, providing the link to your GitHub Pages profile and your LLM Chat Link, along with information about your background and skills.
  3. Initial Screening: Our team will review applications and profile pages. Selected candidates will be contacted for the next stage.
  4. Skills Assessment (if applicable): Depending on the role requirements, you may be asked to complete a brief skills assessment or provide further examples of your work.
  5. Interview: Selected candidates will participate in an interview to discuss their experience and suitability for the role.
  6. Onboarding: Successful applicants will be onboarded and provided with training and resources to become effective Aether Agents.
